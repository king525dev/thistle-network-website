Why we exist

A Scotland where every apprentice — aspiring and current — has the connections and confidence to thrive, professionally and personally.

Support aspiring apprentices

Workshops, mentoring and insight days with employers like Accenture, EY, BT and ScottishPower — focused on apprenticeship pathways, not graduate schemes.

Empower current apprentices

Segmented WhatsApp groups by industry and location, built to replace the social experience often lost by skipping university.

Create real community

Trips, retreats and meet-ups across Scotland, so the network feels like friendship first, networking second.

Build lasting partnerships

Working with Skills Development Scotland, Black Professionals UK, and the Big Career Conference to widen access every year.

Getting involved

Three ways in, no gatekeeping.

01 / Register

Tell us about you

A short Google Form — your role, location and employer or training provider, so we can point you to the right group.

02 / Get matched

We add you to a group

Once approved, you're added to a WhatsApp channel by sector and area — general chat or something more specific.

03 / Get stuck in

Show up, meet people

Insight days, socials, retreats and financial literacy sessions — come to as many or as few as suit you.
